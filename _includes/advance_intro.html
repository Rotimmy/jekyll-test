Advancing the Movement

In the wake of the growing number of challenges we saw in the criminal justice system, we banded together with other innocence organizations, key partners, advocates.

As we strive to truly transform the criminal legal system in this country, we banded together with other innocence organizations, key partners, advocates, and supporters to expand the reach of the innocence movement. In the past year, we launched campaigns to exonerate Sandra Hemme and the West Memphis Three.

To better engage with our audience and amplify our work, we worked alongside our partners to launch a refreshed website that reorganizes our content in easily accessible and searchable ways; includes new sections on the explicit links between race and wrongful conviction and the dire risks of executing innocent people; and allows visitors to better understand our values.
